What are Props?

Props are custom attributes that pass data from parent to child components.

Example:

<app-button type="primary">

Here, "type" is a prop with value "primary"

What are Slots?

Slots allow you to pass content into components.

Example:

<app-button>Click Me</app-button>

"Click Me" is placed in the slot
